## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your **username** (usually your index number)
3. Enter your **password**
4. Click **Login**

> **Note:** If you don't have login credentials, contact your school administrator.

### First Time Login

On your first login, you may need to:
- Change your password
- Complete your profile information
- Verify your contact details

---

## Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your **Dashboard** which shows:
- **Quick Overview** - Summary of your academic information
- **Recent Activities** - Latest updates and notifications
- **Quick Links** - Access to main features

### Navigation Menu

The top navigation bar includes:
- **Dashboard** - Your main page
- **Results** - View your academic results
- **BECE Menu** - Access BECE-related information (if applicable)
- **Profile** - Manage your account settings

## Viewing Results

### Accessing Your Results

1. Click on **Results** in the navigation menu
2. Select your **Program/Class**
3. Choose the **Academic Year** and **Term** (if applicable)
4. Your results will be displayed

### Understanding Your Results

Your results show:
- **Subject Names** - All subjects you're enrolled in
- **Class Marks** - Marks from continuous assessment
- **Exam Marks** - Marks from examinations
- **Total Marks** - Combined class and exam marks
- **Grade** - Letter grade (1-9 scale)
- **Grade Description** - Excellent, Very Good, Good, Credit, Pass, or Fail
- **Position** - Your rank in the class

### Grade Scale

- **Grade 1** - Excellent (80-100%)
- **Grade 2** - Very Good (70-79%)
- **Grade 3** - Good (60-69%)
- **Grade 4-6** - Credit (50-59%)
- **Grade 7-8** - Pass (40-49%)
- **Grade 9** - Fail (Below 40%)

### Printing Results

1. Navigate to your results page
2. Click the **Print** button
3. Your browser's print dialog will open
4. Select your printer and print

> **Note:** Some results may require payment before viewing. If you see a payment prompt, complete the payment first.

## Payment Management

### Viewing Payment Information

1. Click on **Payment Accounts** in the navigation (if available)
2. View your payment history and status

### Making Payments

If payment is required for results or other services:

1. Navigate to the payment page
2. Select the **Payment Type** (Results, Bills, etc.)
3. Enter the **Amount**
4. Choose your **Payment Method** (Paystack)
5. Complete the payment process
6. You'll receive a confirmation

### Payment Status

- **Paid** - Payment completed successfully
- **Pending** - Payment is being processed
- **Failed** - Payment was unsuccessful (try again)

## Profile Management

### Accessing Your Profile

1. Click on your **Profile** in the top-right menu
2. Select **Profile** from the dropdown

### Updating Profile Information

You can update:
- **Email Address**
- **Password**
- **Contact Information** (if allowed)
- **Profile Picture** (if enabled)

### Changing Password

1. Go to **Profile**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your **Current Password**
4. Enter your **New Password**
5. Confirm your **New Password**
6. Click **Update Password**

> **Security Tip:** Use a strong password with letters, numbers, and special characters.

4. View **BECE Results** (when available)

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Problem:** Forgot password or can't access account

**Solution:**
- Contact your school administrator to reset your password
- Ensure you're using the correct username (usually your index number)

### Can't View Results

**Problem:** Results page shows payment required or is blank

**Solution:**
- Complete any required payments
- Ensure results have been published by your school
- Contact your school administrator if issues persist

### Payment Issues

**Problem:** Payment failed or not processing

**Solution:**
- Check your internet connection
- Verify your payment method details
- Try again after a few minutes
- Contact support if payment was deducted but not recorded

### Profile Update Issues

**Problem:** Can't update profile information

**Solution:**
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check that your new password meets requirements (if changing password)
- Contact administrator if certain fields are locked
